Why Partner with LCL Awards

Becoming an LCL Awards approved centre means joining a network built on trust, quality, and shared commitment to excellence. Here's what sets us apart:

  • Established Reputation: Over 20 years of excellence in building services qualifications and certification
  • Comprehensive Support: Dedicated technical and quality assurance teams available throughout your partnership
  • Competitive Pricing: Fair and transparent pricing structure
  • Community Focus: Active engagement through forums, events, and collaboration
  • Quality Management System: Complete QMS templates and documentation ready for your centre to use
  • Free Site Visits: Technical and QMS support visits at no charge to approved centres
  • Training Resources: Extensive training and learner support materials for many courses
  • CPD Opportunities: Regular professional development events for centre staff
  • Assessment Systems: Access to XAMS and Electronic Assessment Programme
  • Portfolio Services: Archiving and scanning service for learner portfolios
